Tante Elke's Pasta Carbonara

This recipe comes all the way from Tante Elke in Austria. It calls for one strange ingredient, Emmental cheese, but if it can be located, the meal is well worth the time and effort put into it.

Ingredients:

  • 10 Strips of Bacon
  • 6 Slices of Prosciutto
  • 1/2 Bag of Frozen Peas
  • 8 oz of Heavy Cream
  • 12 oz Emmental Cheese
  • 2 Eggs

Steps:

  1. Mix together heavy cream, eggs, and cheese together
  2. Add mixture peas, bacon, and prosciutto in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Stir for approximately 5 hours until sauce becomes smooth and semi thick
  4. Serve over pasta
